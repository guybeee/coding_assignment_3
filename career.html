Careers

At Billy's Coffee Shop, we believe in building a team that shares our passion for exceptional coffee and outstanding customer experiences. If you have a love for all things coffee, a warm and welcoming personality, and a desire to be part of a thriving community, we invite you to explore career opportunities with us.

Why Work With Us

  • Passionate Environment: Join a team of coffee enthusiasts who are dedicated to delivering the perfect cup and creating memorable moments for our customers.
  • Training and Development: We invest in the growth of our team members.From barista skills to customer service, we provide comprehensive training to enhance your abilities and build confidence.
  • Community Connection: Billy's Coffee Shop is not just a workplace; it's a community hub. Connect with like-minded individuals and build lasting relationships in a supportive and inclusive environment.
  • Opportunities for Advancement: We value talent and dedication. As we grow, we want our team members to grow with us. Explore opportunities for career advancement within the Billy's family.

Current Open Positions

  • Barista: Craft and serve exceptional coffee creations while providing outstanding customer service.
  • Front-of-House Staff: Welcome and assist customers, ensuring a positive and inviting atmosphere.
